# Project Guidelines

## Default Engineering Rule

Always prioritize scalable, professional, long-term solutions over quick fixes.

### What this means

- Prefer robust architecture over short-term hacks.
- Avoid temporary workarounds unless explicitly requested.
- Make changes with production readiness in mind (security, reliability, maintainability).
- Choose tooling and patterns that scale to more users and developers.
- Ask before taking shortcuts that reduce long-term quality.

## Reminder

If a decision has trade-offs, choose the option that is most stable and future-proof, even if it takes longer.
